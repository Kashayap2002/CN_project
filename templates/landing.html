About Us

We, a team of enthusiastic students from KIIT University, are united by a common purpose: combatting food wastage. Our mission revolves around redistributing surplus, fresh food from local restaurants to offer affordable meals and ensure no one sleeps on an empty stomach.

Driven by a deep concern for the alarming issue of food wastage, our initiative strives to bridge the gap between excess food and those in need. By connecting local eateries with individuals seeking budget-friendly, quality meals, we're determined to minimize food scarcity in our community.

Our commitment lies in fostering a supportive community where everyone has access to nutritious food. Through our efforts, we aim to provide affordable meals, reduce food wastage, and alleviate hunger in our vicinity.

Aim: To provide economical access to quality food and ensure no one faces food insecurity.
